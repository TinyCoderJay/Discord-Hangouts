Discord Hangouts

Summary

Rather than try to update this website frequently to keep up with the changing statistics, I have a bot in my server that compiles all of the servers statistics into an easy to use dashboard for everyone to see. This summary includes member-flow, message count, voice channel count in minutes, and other things too. The bot does have some downtime, which means that it can be inacuate.

Message/Voice Stats

This server at its peak has hit highs of over 4,400 messages in 1 day. That is an extremely impressive amount, and is difficult for servers with thousands of members. Currently, the message average is over 1000 as of the last website update. To see an updated daily message count, Click here.

When it comes to voice channel time, it is much more inconsistent. Some days, the voice channels have been used for over 20 hours in a 24 hour period. Other days, the voice channels have not been used at all. It just depends on what days members decide they want to get together and hangout, play games, or listen to music with a bot.
