Discord Hangouts

About the Server

Discord Hangouts is a community-based Discord server with over 350 members. It was founded on April 27, 2020 by me (WheezyBread63 #0001 on discord). This server has a steady growth rate of about 3-10 members a day. Although the server has this steady growth rate, it has hit highs of 50 new members in one day.

Server Features

  • A friendly Community
  • Excellent Moderation
  • A wide array of well known bots loved by the Discord community
  • New/less popular bots that are also enjoyed by our community
  • An organized layout with a "chill" aesthetic
  • Active text chats and voice channels
  • Easy ways to get special roles and rewards
  • Giveaways for popular things such as Discord Nitro. Both the $5 and the $10 Nitro
  • Special Events like movie night, server creation events, simon says, and more

How it came About

- The way that Discord Hangouts was made is actually quite interesting. I (the creator) used it as a test server (called "Test Server") to try bots, features before adding them to a server that I co-owned with my friend.

- It was his server, but I was made co-owner because of how I improved the server by using my test server. Then, we wanted to increase the member flow. At the time, it was probably 1 new person every 3 days, and some of them left after a glance which of course happens in every growing server.

- So, I looked into Partner Bot. It was a bot that posted your server ad in other servers that had the bot. I added it to my test server to see how it was. After that, I forgot about it and went onto another project.

- A few days later, People started joining my test server. I didn't know how, until I remembered the partner bot. I don't know why people joined a server called "Test Server", but they did. After getting 10 people, and good feedback from them, I decided to remodel it into a proper community server. Since then, it has grown more that I ever thought it would.
